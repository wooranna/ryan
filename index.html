Zeus is the god of the sky, thunder and lightning. He is also King of the Olympians and chief of the gods. his children are Ares, Athena, Apollo, Artemis, Aphrodite, Hephaestus, Hermes, Demeter and Hercules. His wife is Hera, Queen of the Olympians. His parents are Cronus and Rhea.

Poseidon is the god of the Sea, Earthquakes and Horses. His wife is Amphitrite. He always stayed in water rather than Mount Olympus. His children are Perseus, Theseus, Orion and Triton. His symbols are a Horse and a Three pronged trident.

Hades is the god of the underworld and riches. His wife is persephone, daughter of Demeter. His parents are Cronus and Rhea. He has a three headed dog named Cerberus. Cerberus gaurds the gates of the underworld. His brothers are Zeus and Poseidon. His symbols are Cerberus, Sypress tree, a Skull and a Drinking Horn.

Hephaestus is the god of metalwork and fire. His symbols are an anvil, hammer and tongs. His children are Thalia and Eucleia. His parents are Zeus and Hera. His wife is Aphrodite.   

Athena is the god of wisdom and strategy. Her symbols are a shield, owl, serpent, armour, olive tree and spear. Her parents are Zeus and Metis. 

Artemis is the god of wilderness. Her symbols are bow and arrow, hunting dog and the moon. Her parents are Zeus and Leto. her twin brother is apollo. 

Ares is the god of war and violence. His symbols are a spear, helmet, dog, vuture and boar. Ares’ children are Phobos, Deimos and Harmonia. Ares’ parents are Zeus and Hera. His roman name is Mars. 

Hera is the god of marriage. Her husband and brother is Zeus, king of the Olympians. Hera is also Queen of the Olympians. Her parents are Cronus and Rhea. 

Dionysus is the god of wine. His symbols are grapevine, drinking cup and ivy. His parents are Zeus and semele. His children are priapus and maron. His Roman name is Bacchus.
